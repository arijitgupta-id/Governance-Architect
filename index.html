Philosophy & Board Value

Governance as a strategic asset

"Strong governance does not slow growth — it protects, sharpens and sustains it."

As a Certified Independent Director and global compliance leader, I work at the intersection of board oversight, regulatory complexity, and scalable business growth. I help boards anticipate risk before it becomes reputational, regulatory, or operational crisis.

With over 21 years of leadership experience across the UK, India, and Korea, I have built and overseen multi-jurisdictional governance and compliance programs within high-growth enterprises. Currently Head of Compliance at Coupang Fulfillment Services, and formerly Sr. Director & Head – Regulatory Compliance at Coupang Corp. and Deputy Chief Ethics & Compliance Officer at Flipkart, I have guided organisations through regulatory expansion, operational scale, and heightened scrutiny across diverse markets.

Three anchoring principles
01
Proactive risk mitigation creates competitive advantage.
02
Governance frameworks should enable strategy — not obstruct it.
03
Sustainable value creation depends on ethical leadership and structured oversight.
Board value proposition
  • Strengthening governance frameworks before inflection points
  • Embedding proactive risk oversight into growth strategy
  • Enhancing audit, risk, and compliance committee effectiveness
  • Translating regulatory complexity into board-level clarity
  • Building ethical, resilient, and future-ready organisations

Boards that de-risk early lead markets. Those that respond only after crisis are forced into defensive governance.

As an Independent Director, I contribute structured thinking, global regulatory insight, and operational realism — ensuring governance is not a reporting exercise, but a strategic asset.

I welcome opportunities to serve on boards and committees where governance maturity, risk intelligence, and sustainable growth are priorities.
